Simple Omelette Recipe

Cooked until fluffy, it can be filled with your favorite ingredients like cheese or veggies.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or cooking oil
  • Optional fillings such as cheese, vegetables, ham, etc.

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a mixing bowl. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Beat the eggs with a fork or whisk until well blended and slightly frothy.
  3. Heat the butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat until melted and hot.
  4. Pour the beaten eggs into the skillet, swirling the pan to evenly distribute the eggs.
  5. Let the eggs cook undisturbed for about 1-2 minutes, until the edges start to set.
  6. Using a spatula, gently lift the edges of the omelette and tilt the skillet to allow any uncooked eggs to flow underneath.
  7. Once the omelette is mostly set but still slightly runny on top, add your desired fillings evenly over one half of the omelette.
  8. Carefully fold the other half of the omelette over the fillings, creating a half-moon shape.
  9. Cook for another 1-2 minutes until the omelette is fully set and lightly golden on the outside.
  10. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve hot.
